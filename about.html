He Singer and The Strings

In a corner, dim-lit, where the shadows fall,A singer strums, and the notes recall the whispers of winds, the hum of the sea, A tale untold, wild and free

Fingers dance upon silver strings,Each pluck a story, each strum it sings.The guitar hums like an old, dear friend, Together they weave songs that never end

His voice, a river, flows soft and low, Through valleys of sorrow, where melodies grow. And in the echoes of the chords he plays, The night turns to music in endless ways.

soul unburdened, he lets it fly, As the guitar lifts his heart to the sky. For in every note, in each refrain, The singer finds peace, again and again
